Startup Advisory · 2015

Gyroscope Growth/Product

Helped the quantified-self startup evolve from a data visualization tool into a subscription health companion.

Focus

Gyroscope aggregated fitness, sleep, and productivity data into beautiful lifelog dashboards. My role was to craft the product loops and messaging that would convert curious users into paying members.

Key contributions

  • Mapped the customer journey, identifying drop-off points between data import, daily engagement, and habit formation.
  • Designed onboarding flows that surfaced “magic moments” within the first 48 hours—like highlighting streaks or unexpected correlations.
  • Prototyped subscription paywalls that focused on personal coaching, weekly reports, and accountability nudges.
  • Partnered with founders on messaging experiments across email, landing pages, and in-app storytelling.

Results

  • Increased activation by 21% through the new onboarding flows.
  • Established a repeatable launch cadence for new data integrations (RescueTime, Withings) that drove acquisition spikes.
  • Laid groundwork for the Gyroscope X membership program that later became the company’s primary revenue stream.

Influence on my work

This engagement sharpened my growth instincts and reminded me how critical narrative is when translating raw data into meaningful action—a lesson that carries into my AI work today.
