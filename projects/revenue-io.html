AI Product Leadership · 2021–2023

Revenue.io

Scaled AI copilots for sales and success teams, pairing real-time guidance with compliant conversation intelligence.

Engagement snapshot

  • Role: Director of Product, AI
  • Team: 45-person product, design, data science, and engineering org across LA and Buenos Aires
  • Customers: Mid-market and enterprise GTM teams in healthcare, finance, and SaaS

The mandate

Revenue leaders needed more than call recording—they wanted copilots that could coach reps mid-conversation, automate follow-up, and drive forecast accuracy. We had to deliver trustworthy experiences while respecting privacy, compliance, and CRM realities.

What I led

  • Shipped "Moments"—a streaming inference system that flags risk topics (competitors, pricing, objections) within 2 seconds and serves playbook prompts.
  • Launched a Conversation Intelligence workspace that summarized calls, drafted CRM updates, and suggested coaching actions.
  • Introduced quality gates: bias testing harnesses, human QA workflows, and an explainability UI exposing transcript excerpts and model rationale.
  • Rebuilt roadmap rituals connecting sales engineers, customer success, and research to close the loop on adoption.

Impact

  • Increased meeting conversion by 18% for teams adopting Moments across the full funnel.
  • Cut manual CRM data entry by 11 hours per rep each month via automated summaries and follow-ups.
  • Expanded annual recurring revenue by 36% year-over-year while maintaining SOC 2 and HIPAA compliance.

Why it matters

This work cemented my POV on human-centered AI. The highest-performing teams didn’t replace managers—they gave them signal-rich insights and shared language to coach. That philosophy now underpins every advisory engagement I take on.
