AI Incubation · 2024

GenCog

Built a generation-aware cognition engine that fuses institutional knowledge with live model reasoning for enterprise PM teams.

Engagement snapshot

  • Role: Founding product lead and interim head of product
  • Team: 7-person strike team across applied research, platform engineering, and design
  • Customers: Fortune 500 product orgs and boutique consultancies needing faster synthesis

The challenge

Teams were drowning in product docs, customer interviews, and competitive intel. Traditional knowledge bases went stale immediately, while direct LLM usage hallucinated confidently. We needed a system that respected provenance, captured evolving context, and worked in compliance-heavy environments.

What we shipped

  • Designed the GenCog schema that tags every artifact with actors, temporal context, and model confidence to power timeline-aware retrieval.
  • Led prompt and agent architecture experiments combining deterministic pipelines with human-in-the-loop checkpoints.
  • Created the product surface—a research workspace with "ask, compare, and brief" modes plus citation-first responses.
  • Instrumented governance: red-teaming scripts, audit logs, and workspace-level permissions for regulated clients.

Impact

  • Reduced synthesis time for pilot customers by 63%, compressing a week of research into a morning.
  • Unlocked deal velocity: closed three six-figure design partner agreements in the first quarter.
  • Established reusable evaluation harnesses now leveraged by my UCLA course and advisory clients.

What I learned

Trustworthy AI is as much about experience design as it is about model quality. By exposing provenance, confidence, and escalation paths in the UI, we turned skeptics into daily power users. Those patterns now inform every copilot engagement I touch.
