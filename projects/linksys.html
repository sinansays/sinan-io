Smart Home · 2015–2018

Linksys

Built the software experiences behind Linksys’ mesh networking lineup, making Wi-Fi setup and management approachable.

Engagement snapshot

  • Role: Product lead for Linksys app and cloud services
  • Team: Firmware, cloud platform, design, security, and support teams across LA, Taipei, and Bangalore
  • Devices: Velop mesh routers, gaming routers, and small business gateways

Problems to solve

Networking hardware was powerful but intimidating. Customers needed fast setup, trustworthy security, and proactive diagnostics without reading a manual.

Highlights

  • Designed the Velop onboarding flow that auto-detected nodes, optimized placement, and finished setup in under 5 minutes.
  • Shipped Security Center: intrusion detection, parental controls, and device quarantine flows backed by Trend Micro intelligence.
  • Built remote management features for installers and prosumers, including scheduled reboots, firmware orchestration, and alerts.
  • Instrumented telemetry dashboards that informed hardware roadmap decisions and reduced support escalations.

Impact

  • Achieved a 4.7★ average rating in the App Store within six months of launch.
  • Cut setup-related support calls by 55% compared to prior router generations.
  • Enabled subscription security services, opening a new recurring revenue stream for Belkin International.

Reflection

The Linksys work reinforced the importance of translating deep technical value into human language. It remains a reference project whenever I build consumer-facing admin tools.
