Enterprise SaaS · 2018–2020

Dealpath

Led product and UX for Dealpath’s core platform, powering billions in commercial real estate transactions.

Engagement snapshot

  • Role: Director of Product Management
  • Team: Product trio model (PM, design, engineering) embedded with data science and customer success
  • Customers: Institutional investors, REITs, and private equity funds

Strategic focus

Dealpath sat at the center of the CRE deal lifecycle. As customers scaled, they needed deeper underwriting automation, better collaboration across acquisitions and asset management, and portfolio-level insight for executives.

Key releases

  • Launched underwriting templates with scenario modeling, permissions, and audit trails that satisfied Big Four compliance reviews.
  • Introduced portfolio analytics dashboards that surfaced exposure, pipeline velocity, and capital deployment pacing.
  • Created APIs and integrations for Salesforce, Box, and Argus to reduce swivel-chair work.
  • Overhauled onboarding and in-app guidance, shortening time-to-first-deal from 45 to 19 days.

Impact

  • Doubled enterprise ACV by expanding into asset management and investor relations workflows.
  • Improved data quality across customer portfolios, enabling benchmarking products and new monetization lines.
  • Helped secure Series C fundraising by demonstrating platform stickiness and predictive insights.

Legacy

The discipline we built around permissions, change management, and customer success continues to inform how I scale enterprise products—especially when data accuracy drives real dollars.
