UCLA AI for PM Course

Quick Snapshot

  • Role: Course Creator & Instructor
  • Team: Collaborated with UCLA Extension administrators, faculty, and guest experts
  • Customers: Working professionals and product leaders reskilling in AI product management

Mandate / Opportunity / Problem Scope

I designed UCLA Extension’s first “AI for Product Management” course to equip product teams with a practical framework for spotting, scoping, and shipping AI-powered solutions. The program bridges strategy, responsible development, and delivery rigor for students bringing projects back to employers like Apple, Disney, NBCUniversal, Microsoft, Boeing, and Northrop.

What I Led / Delivered / Highlights

  • Developed the course curriculum in 2024 and have continuously taught it since Winter ’25.
  • Guided student capstone projects end-to-end, helping them translate course frameworks into real initiatives inside their companies.
  • Scaled enrollment by 150% quarter-over-quarter through 2025, prompting UCLA to expand sections for 2026.
  • Advised UCLA Extension leadership on AI certificate design, industry trends, and governance considerations.
  • Delivered public seminars such as “Spotting AI Opportunities: A Framework for Product Management” to extend the program’s reach.

Impact / Lasting Value / Takeaway

The course has driven more than $20M in downstream P&L impact across student employers and established a repeatable way for UCLA to help professionals accelerate trustworthy AI adoption.
