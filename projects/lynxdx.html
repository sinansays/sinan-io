Healthcare Platform · 2020–2021

LynxDx

Led the product transformation of a diagnostics scale-up as it expanded from COVID testing to oncology and urology lines.

Engagement snapshot

  • Role: Head of Product (fractional)
  • Team: Cross-functional squad of 18 spanning engineering, lab ops, support, and revenue cycle
  • Customers: Health systems, employer programs, and direct-to-consumer patients

The opportunity

LynxDx experienced hypergrowth during the pandemic, exposing brittle internal tooling. Providers lacked visibility into order status, lab techs were triaging spreadsheets, and leadership needed clearer payer and compliance reporting.

What I delivered

  • Shipped a unified ordering portal with eligibility checks, insurance capture, and kiosk mode for drive-through sites.
  • Introduced a logistics control tower that tracked specimens from pickup to lab accessioning, integrating with FedEx and in-house courier APIs.
  • Built payer dashboards summarizing claim status, denials, and medical necessity documentation.
  • Established governance rituals (backlog council, lab floor stand-ups, QA sign-off) to maintain quality while shipping weekly.

Impact

  • Improved turnaround time from 72 to 28 hours by eliminating manual reconciliation steps.
  • Reduced support tickets by 45% through proactive status alerts and clearer patient communication.
  • Enabled the launch of three new test lines without adding net-new headcount to operations.

Lasting value

The platform upgrades positioned LynxDx to pivot beyond pandemic testing and secure longer-term payer contracts. The experience reinforced how product can bridge lab science, regulatory requirements, and customer empathy.
