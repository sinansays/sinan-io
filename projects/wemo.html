Smart Home · 2016–2019

Wemo

Unified Belkin’s Wemo ecosystem—from smart plugs and dimmers to app automations—around reliability and clear control.

Engagement snapshot

  • Role: Group product manager
  • Team: Firmware, mobile, cloud, and industrial design partners across US, Mexico, and China
  • Devices: Smart plugs, light switches, dimmers, and sensors

What needed fixing

Customers loved Wemo’s flexibility but struggled with flaky automations and inconsistent app flows. We needed to rebuild trust while preparing for emerging standards like HomeKit, Alexa Routines, and Matter.

Highlights

  • Redesigned the Wemo app with a scenes-first IA, contextual device cards, and fast actions for power users.
  • Shipped Reliability Mode—on-device schedules with cloud sync failover—cutting missed automations dramatically.
  • Launched privacy controls that clarified when cloud connectivity was required and allowed local-only operation.
  • Led the multi-platform certification work for Apple HomeKit, Google Assistant, Amazon Alexa, and early Matter pilots.

Impact

  • Boosted automation success rate from 82% to 97% through Reliability Mode and better device health monitoring.
  • Increased app retention by 29% thanks to streamlined onboarding and contextual education.
  • Positioned Wemo as a launch partner for Matter, ensuring interoperability and future-proofing the portfolio.

What stuck with me

Wemo taught me the craft of blending industrial design, firmware, and cloud reliability. Those lessons continue to inform how I approach multi-surface AI assistants today.
