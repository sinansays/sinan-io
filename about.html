About

At my core, I celebrate the hacker ethos: curiosity, craftsmanship, and a love for tinkering and clever solutions, always with a dash of humility and humor. Career Outcomes

I lead teams from early 0→1 through adoption, with strong impact across GenAI, B2B SaaS, health-tech, and IoT. My selected outcomes include:

  • Teaching UCLA’s first "AI for Product Management" course, with 150% QoQ registration growth in 2025 and students' AI capstone projects driving >$20M/yr downstream P&L impact at employers.
  • At Revenue.io, I shipped LLM-powered GenAI features ('Ask Revenue’ chatbot, Conversation Highlights, GenAI Scorecards, etc.) that produced 5x engagement lift at launch.
  • At LynxDx, I hired and led an 8-person eng/IT/systems team to scale-up lab ops software that won us public-sector contracts exceeding $100M in the highly regulated healthcare/diagnostics environment (HIPAA/CAP/CLIA/NIST).
  • At Dealpath, I supported >$10T in transactions evaluated on-platform by shipping 10+ workflow features (some +250% MoM growth) with cited customer impact of 5+ hours/week saved/person, >$1B+ closed/committed (and $8.5B closed for a marquee client in 2021).
  • At Belkin’s Linksys and Wemo divisions, my research informed smart home launches that won PCMag’s Best Smart Plug 2018, Wirecutter’s Best Smart Plug 2019, and the CES 2019 TWICE Picks Award for the Wemo 3-way Light Switch.
